# Reflection on Music Recommender Simulation

## Profile Comparisons

- **High-Energy Pop vs. Chill Lofi**: The high-energy profile pushes intense, upbeat tracks like "Gym Hero" to the top because of matching energy and mood, while chill lofi favors mellow, low-energy songs like "Midnight Coding." This shows energy is a strong signal for activity level—high energy for workouts, low for relaxation.

- **Deep Intense Rock vs. Adversarial Pop Chill High Energy**: Rock profile loves heavy, intense tracks, but the adversarial one mixes pop style with chill mood and high energy, causing "Gym Hero" to win because genre beats mood conflict. It makes sense for style to dominate, but surprises when mood is ignored.

- **Edge Case Jazz vs. Others**: Jazz profile ranks "Coffee Shop Stories" first due to genre match, proving the system adapts to rare genres, but relies on energy when genres don't match, keeping results relevant.

The "Gym Hero" song keeps showing up for happy pop users because it matches genre and has high energy, which aligns with pop's upbeat nature. The system captures basic vibe matching well but could improve diversity.
